PROJECTS

I love creating projects from scratch, especially ones that are meant to help people. While making them, I make sure that my projects have readable and clean code, are easily accessible open-source and are of use to the tech community that has helped me whenever I was (am) struggling.
Most of these projects have either been made from self interest or curiousity, some for MLH or non-MLH hackathons, some for open source and others for work. Let me know what you think of them and how I can improve them! :)

Every project is an opportunity to learn, to figure out problems and challenges, to invent and reinvent.
